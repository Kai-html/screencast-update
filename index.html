My Screencasting Tips!




Before:




 1. Create a small checklist of things you want to say,
so you don't forget.


 2. Close out of any tabs/applications/desktops that 
complicate your ability to record your screen.


 3. Adjust your mic volume and your computer volume. Test them.


 4. Adjust the size and position of your facecam.


 5. Clear your throat.






During:




 1. Act natural and speak clearly.


 2. The cursor is a good tool to use alongside
your speech to talk about different things on the screen.
